Share information about travel throughout South America

There are plenty places to read about this topic, but my blog will not just be about traveling. I will be writing many posts about how travel affects life and happiness in general. I want to help people understand the possibility of travelling and invoke thoughts and ideas.


Discuss high-level topics on life

The topics of happiness and meaning of life have always captivated my interest. Finding meaning in life is something many people struggle with and it changes over time. There is no correct answer and everyone finds happiness and meaning in their lives in their own personal ways. I will discuss how I find happiness and maybe that will shed glimpses of light on fellow readers and followers.
